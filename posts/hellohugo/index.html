hello hugo

2021-06-02 214 words 2 minutes
Contents

“Hello World”, or should I say “Hello Hugo.”

I’ve wanted to start blogging again for sometime now.

What do they say?

The best time to start a blog was 30 years ago, the next best time is today.

… or something like that.

I’m playing around with a few hugo themes tonight. Hopefully I’ll manage to at least keep the content going. My intention is to keep this content short. My inspiration is something akin to the Today I Learned (til) format. Something I can utilize to reference things I learned, or found useful, or just wanted to share in that moment of time, like now.

So, here’s to a new begining using Hugo. I’m sure a blog post could accompany this migration to hugo, but there are already plenty of those out there. Mine is not unique. I was looking for a easy way to deploy a blog in public that deploys on push to main. Simple.

Originally I wanted to create the site in pure Go, but have opted to use Hugo for now as it seems to have a large community, is written in Go, and again see above about deployment ease (free as well).

Never going to make that mistake again.
